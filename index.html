Transfer Prescriptions

Call one of our eight convenient locations today to transfer your prescriptions! Give us your name, the prescription number, and the phone number of the pharmacy where your prescription was previously filled.

Refills

We take refill requests by phone or email and just need your name and the prescription number or name of the medication. We offer free online refills through refillrx.com and mobile refills through the MyGNP mobile apps, available for iOS and Android.

About Us

Med One Pharmacy is a family-owned independent pharmacy chain. We offer friendly, personalized service that specifically caters to your individual needs. You'll also find the best value in pricing, selection, and support. As a full-service pharmacy, we carry a complete line of inventory to cover all of your prescription needs and accept most insurance companies.
